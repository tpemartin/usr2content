高齡社區照顧微學程
by 陳以瑋, 2019-01-15 13:11, Views(1852)
英文名稱：Senior community care micro program
 1.學程簡介
教育目標：因應超高齡社會的來臨，培育具有長期照顧和社區工作知識的基礎人才。
In response to the advent of the super-aged  society, the aim of this micro-program is to cultivate the talents  with knowledge in long-term care services and community work. 
取得證書所須修習學分數：8學分。

 The number of credits required to obtain the certificate: 8 credits
